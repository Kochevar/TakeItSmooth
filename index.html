Smoothie

Our smoothies are made from high quality raw fruit and vegetables. They don’t contain any added sugar and animal-based ingredients. This delicious and refreshing drink will energize you and provide you with vitamins and minerals that you need for your body functions. They are also rich source of dietary fibre that will improve your digestion and antioxidants that will protect you from numerous diseases.

Raw Dessert

You want to be fit, but you have a sweet-tooth? With our raw deserts, you don’t have to give up sweets! Our raw deserts are an excellent substitute for all chocolate-bars and cakes that make you feel tired and increase your risk of developing diseases such as type 2 diabetes. They contain no added sugars and they also don’t contain milk, butter or any other animal-based ingredients. Instead, they are made from high quality fruits, nuts and gluten-free cereals. In one word – healthilicious!

Fruit or vegetable salad

We offer fruit-based and vegetable-based salads. These all-plant based and low-calorie dishes are high in beneficial nutrients and they are an excellent choice for anyone who wants to be fit and improve his health. Our salads contain no added sugar and they are low in sodium and fat. On the top, they contain spices that, not only make them delicious, but also provide you with numerous powerful antioxidants.
